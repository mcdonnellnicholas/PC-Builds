Here you will find various PC builds I have done over the years along with parts specifications and details.



All Black RGB PC w/ Ryzen 5 4500, GTX 1660 Super 6GB





This build was made in late 2024 as a budget gaming PC from a budget of $500. It is capable of running most games at 1080p high settings with good framerates. The parts list is as follows:

  • Ryzen 5 4500 6-core, 12-thread
  • GTX 1660 Super 6GB VRAM
  • 16GB DDR4 3200Mhz XPG RGB
  • ASrock B450M-HDV R4.0 AM4 Socket
  • Apevia SPIRIT600W Spirit 600W ATX PSU
  • EDUP PCI-E WiFi 6 Network Card
  • Cooler Master MasterBox Q300L Micro-ATX Tower
  • Thermalright TL-C12C-S X3 CPU Fans + ARGB fan hub

All White RGB PC w/ Ryzen 5 5600, RTX 3070 8GB, 16GB DDR4 3200Mhz



This build was made in early 2025 as a mid-range gaming PC from a budget of $800. It is capable of running all games at 1080p ultra quality presets or most games at 1440p high settings with excellent framerates. The parts list is as follows:


  • Ryzen 5 5500 6-core, 12-thread
  • RTX 3070 8GB VRAM
  • 32GB DDR4 3600Mhz RGB XPG
  • ASrock B550M AM4 Socket
  • Apevia SPIRIT 700W ATX PSU
  • 1TB KLEVV gen4x4 M.2 SSD
  • EDUP PCI-E WiFi 6 Network Card
  • SAMA White Micro-ATX Tower
  • Thermalright TL-C12CW-S X3 CPU Fan 120mm White ARGB Case Cooler
  • Sirlyr RGB 8 Pin PCIE Cable, 3 x 8Pin RGB GPU PSU Cables

Budget $350 PC Build w/ Ryzen 5 3600, GTX 1660 Super 6GB, 16GB DDR4 3000Mhz



Here is a look at a custom build I did for a friend on a TIGHT $350 budget during the summer of 2025. I wanted to make sure he had some RGB lighting and good performance for this price so that he could play the newest games with us. This build is capable of running esports titles at 1080p max settings with great framerates, or most games at 1080p medium settings at 60-80fps. The parts list is as follows:


  • Ryzen 5 3600 6-core, 12-thread
  • GTX 1660 Super 6GB VRAM
  • 16GB DDR4 3200Mhz XPG
  • ASrock B450M-HDV R4.0 AM4 Socket
  • Apevia SPIRIT600W Spirit 600W ATX PSU
  • 500GB KLEVV gen3x4 M.2 SSD
  • 2TB Seagate HDD
  • Ali-Express $38 no-brand White PC Case
  • Thermalright TL-C12C-S X3 CPU Fans + ARGB fan hub
